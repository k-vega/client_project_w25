Your Comprehensive Guide to Resources for First Generation Student Success

This guide is set up to help you be successul inside and outside of the classroom as a first generation student and we have included the following resources throughout our site:

  1. Study Spaces
  2. Tutoring
  3. Connecting with First Gen at the University

Study Spaces

Having spaces where you can focus is essential for success. The University of Michigan offers numerous spaces where you can sit down, relax, and do any school work whether you are alone or with friends:

Mason Hall

Mason Hall is one of the many places on campus where you can study. With access to Angell Hall, it provides versatile environments to suit your study needs.

Tutoring

Maintaining passing grades is crucial for thriving here at UMSI. That is why we provide a resource to support your academics:

UMSI Tutoring

As a programming and math heavy major, you have access to individual tutoring in these key areas to support your learning.

First Gen at the University

  • Find Others: There are many students at the University of Michigan who may come from similar backgrounds. Learn about their stories and make it a goal to meet others!
  • First-Gen Gateway: If you do not know where to start with connecting with First Gen, the Gateway may be the perfect spot for you to learn more!
  • Events: First Gen holds events where you can meet others just like you! Attend any of them to connect with others and have a good time.
  • And More: First Gen offers multiple resources to help you feel more comfortable here at the university.
