First Gen at the University of Michigan

At the University of Michigan, we recognize the unique experiences and challenges faced by first-generation college students—those who are the first in their families to attend university. Whether you're just starting your journey or are already immersed in the college experience, this space is here for you.

Events

Come take part in the First Gen Winter Community Dinner! You're part of a community that shares your experiences and understands your challenges. We encourage you to connect with fellow first-gen students, find mentorship, and build relationships that will support both your academic and personal growth throughout your time at Michigan.

First-Gen Gateway

It’s okay to ask for help. It’s okay to seek guidance. U-M is more than just a place to study—it’s a place where you can grow, succeed, and feel a sense of belonging. Whether you need help adjusting to college life, managing course loads, or planning your future, we’re here to help you thrive. Housed in the Office of Academic Multicultural Initiatives (OAMI), the First Generation Student Gateway serves as a starting point to get connected to resources for first-generation students.
